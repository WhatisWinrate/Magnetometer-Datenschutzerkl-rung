Datenschutzerklärung Magnetometer

Privacy Policy – Magnetometer App  Last updated: 9 March 2026  1. General Information  This Privacy Policy explains how the Magnetometer App handles information when you use the application.  Protecting user privacy is important. The app is designed to minimize data processing and does not collect personal information from users.  This policy is intended to comply with applicable data protection regulations including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).  2. External Sensor Device  The Magnetometer App is designed to work with a dedicated external magnetometer device.  The application receives measurement data from this external hardware in order to display magnetic field values.  The received measurement data:  is processed only locally on the user's device  is not stored permanently  is not transmitted to external servers  is not shared with third parties  The data received from the external device consists solely of physical measurement values and does not contain personal information.  3. Personal Data  The Magnetometer App does not collect, store, process, or transmit personal data.  In particular, the app does not collect:  location data  contact information  device identifiers  user accounts  analytics data  4. Internet Connectivity  The application does not require an internet connection and does not transmit any user data to external services.  5. Third-Party Services  The app does not use third-party services that collect user data.  6. User Rights  Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users generally have rights including:  the right to access personal data  the right to rectification  the right to erasure  the right to restrict processing  the right to data portability  the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority  However, since the app does not process personal data, these rights generally do not apply in practice.  7. Changes to This Privacy Policy  This Privacy Policy may be updated if the functionality of the application changes or if legal requirements change. Updates will be reflected by modifying the "Last updated" date.
